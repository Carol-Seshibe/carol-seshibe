Our Journey

BlackStar Beverages is a beverage manufacturing company, in Pretoria, South Africa. Founded in 2022 by Matshela Charles Seshibe, the company is dedicated to innovate by crafting refreshing and flavourful beverages. Since then, the company introduced its first brand to the drinks scene, Ginnoir. Ginnoir is a gin and tonic blend, marketed by BlackStar as a premium spirit cooler.

Our Values

We centre ourselves around being premium, drinks manufacturer with carefully selected ingredients, and a local production that adds that South African authenticity. Our main messaging emphasizes a refreshing experience, positioning ourselves as a perfect summer companion for special occasions and hangouts.
